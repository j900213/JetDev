This is my first day journaling.

And this is the second day after I was kicked out from MULTI project.

I knew it would come, but when it came, it hurt. Since the beginning of January, I started feeling that I was not welcomed anymore in this team: Luuk ignored my messages, Matthias lost patience when answering my questions. My work on RPC discovery was not appreciated at all. Every question I asked exposed a tip of my ignorance to them. And was yesterday, I was asked to get out there. I did feel unfair as they didn't seems to give me more chances: they started throwing trivial and random tasks to me since they found I did not perform well at the first task, fuck.

Ruud was a nice guy. But the more kind he tried to be to me, the more miserable I seem to be. I've been always telling myself that I am not a smart guy, but when others tell me that in my face, I am upset. It seems I don't truly believe I am not smart, I want to prove so bad, but I keep on failing.

Think positively: one good news is that I have a second chance to prove myself in a different team, I can't fail again. Maybe I always set a low standard to myself and didn't realize it, it might be a good thing that someone pointed that out for me (and slap on my face).

The other good news is that I am not that kind of person who gets stuck at sadness, I felt much better today. Life is not going at the direction where I want it to be, but what else can I do? Things already happened, and I need to look forward.

So, Journaling is the thing that I think I can do for a month, in order to be more mindful: telling what I am feeling to myself, no matter it's good or bad, face it. This will be my first English journal, there is no reason why I write in English, I just want to challenge myself.

I will talk to you tomorrow Jet, hope you will be less ignorant 24 hours later.
